About Me


Hello! My name is Lesley Pond. I am a student at the UNC Coding Bootcamp. Thanks for checking out my portfolio! I currently work as a bartender at Dain's Place, a local Durham favorite. I am also an avid musician and have spent the last several years recording and performing under the pseudonym Janxx. You can check out some of my musical work at JanxxMusic.com. I discovered my interest in coding through a fellow musician, and since then have been working to develop my skills. I love the freedom and creativity web development allows! For some examples of my work please follow the Portfolio link, and feel free to contact me anytime for more information!
